CYBERPUNK 2077

1st gameplay , Aug 24th, 2025

Cyberpunk 2077, developed by CD Projekt Red, is an ambitious open-world RPG set in the sprawling futuristic metropolis of Night City. Blending dystopian storytelling with deep role-playing systems, the game offers players the chance to carve their own path through a world dominated by corporations, gangs, and cybernetic enhancement.

You play as V, a mercenary trying to survive and make a name in Night City. The central narrative revolves around a mysterious biochip and the digital ghost of rocker-turned-terrorist Johnny Silverhand, portrayed by Keanu Reeves. The story explores themes of identity, transhumanism, and corporate control. Many side quests are just as memorable as the main plot, featuring well-written characters and moral choices that often lack clear “good” outcomes.

ELDEN RING

1st gameplay, March 4th, 2025

Elden Ring, developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment, is a massive open-world action RPG that blends the brutal challenge of the Souls series with a vast fantasy world. Directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki and featuring worldbuilding contributions from George R. R. Martin, the game transports players to the haunting realm of The Lands Between.

The story centers on restoring the shattered Elden Ring and becoming the Elden Lord. However, the narrative is intentionally vague and fragmented. Much of the lore is hidden in item descriptions, NPC dialogue, and environmental clues. This cryptic storytelling style is typical of FromSoftware games. Some players love the mystery and theory-crafting, while others find it confusing or difficult to follow.
